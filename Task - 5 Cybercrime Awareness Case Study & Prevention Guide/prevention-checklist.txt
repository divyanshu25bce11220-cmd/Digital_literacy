# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## For College Students in India

---

### ✅ Actionable Prevention Tips

| # | Prevention Tip |
|---|----------------|
| **1** | **Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or debit/credit card details** with anyone — even if they claim to be a bank employee. |
| **2** | **Verify caller identity** — Banks never ask for OTP or PIN over phone. Hang up and call the official bank number if unsure. |
| **3** | **Never install screen-sharing apps** (AnyDesk, TeamViewer) when requested by unknown callers. |
| **4** | **Check UPI transaction alerts immediately** — If you receive an unexpected OTP, never share it. |
| **5** | **Use strong, unique passwords** for all online accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA). |
| **6** | **Avoid clicking on suspicious links** received via SMS, email, or WhatsApp claiming lottery wins, refunds, or urgent KYC updates. |
| **7** | **Keep your devices updated** — Regularly update phone and app software to receive security patches. |
| **8** | **Set transaction limits** on your UPI apps to minimize potential loss in case of fraud. |

---

### 💰 Financial/UPI Safety Tips (Specific)

| # | Tip |
|---|-----|
| **1** | **Enable UPI PIN for every transaction** — Never use apps that allow auto-debit without PIN confirmation. |
| **2** | **Check recipient name before transferring money** — On Google Pay and PhonePe, always verify the recipient's name before confirming payment. |

---

### 📞 Reporting Channels

| Channel | Details |
|---------|--------|
| **National Cyber Crime Portal** | https://cybercrime.gov.in — File a complaint online |
| **Cyber Crime Helpline** | **1930** — Call immediately after fraud occurs |
| **Bank Helpline** | Block your card/report to your bank immediately |

---

### 🚨 What to Do If You Are a Victim

1. Stay calm — don't panic
2. Call **1930** immediately to report
3. Visit https://cybercrime.gov.in and file a complaint
4. Block your UPI account or debit card through your bank app
5. Save all evidence (screenshots, transaction IDs, caller numbers)
