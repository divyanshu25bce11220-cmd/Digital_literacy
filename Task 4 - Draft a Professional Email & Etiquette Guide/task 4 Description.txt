# Task 4: Email Etiquette & Digital Communication

## Digital Ambassador Responsibility

As a Digital Ambassador, one of your key responsibilities is teaching your peers how to communicate professionally online. This task tests your ability to:

1. Apply email etiquette yourself by drafting professional emails
2. Communicate those rules clearly to others through a structured checklist

---

## Part A: Professional Emails

Write two professional emails following all standard email etiquette rules.

### Email 1: Student to Professor – Assignment Extension Request

| Element | Requirement |
|---------|-------------|
| Subject | Clear and specific |
| Greeting | Professional (e.g., "Dear Professor [Name],") |
| Body | Polite request, brief honest reason, respect for professor's time |
| Sign-off | Proper closing with full name and relevant details |

### Email 2: Student to Internship Coordinator – Summer Internship Interest

| Element | Requirement |
|---------|-------------|
| Subject | Clear with purpose and name |
| Greeting | Professional (e.g., "Dear [Name/Hiring Team],") |
| Body | Introduction, expression of interest, relevant skills, call to action |
| Sign-off | Proper closing with name, contact details, and links |
