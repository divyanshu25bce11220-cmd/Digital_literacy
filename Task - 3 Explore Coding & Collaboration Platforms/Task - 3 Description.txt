QUIZ QUESTIONS:

1. What does "digital literacy" primarily mean?
A) Knowing how to play video games
B) The ability to use, understand, and create digital content safely and effectively
C) Having the latest smartphone or gadget
D) Only knowing how to browse the internet
Correct Answer: B

2. Which of the following is the best example of practicing good digital citizenship?
A) Sharing someone’s private photos without permission
B) Respecting others’ opinions online and avoiding cyberbullying
C) Using someone else’s Wi-Fi without asking
D) Posting fake news to get more likes
Correct Answer: B

3. What is phishing?
A) A type of online shopping
B) A cyber attack where attackers trick you into revealing personal information through fake emails or websites
C) A method to increase internet speed
D) A new social media trend
Correct Answer: B

4. Which action shows strong digital literacy when evaluating online information?
A) Believing everything you read on social media
B) Checking the source, date, and credibility of the information before sharing
C) Sharing a post because it has many likes
D) Only reading headlines
Correct Answer: B

Short Answer Question:
5. Explain in 2-3 sentences why digital literacy is important in today’s world and give one personal example of how you apply it in your daily life.
