# Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

**Tool Used:** Canva (canva.com)

**Description:**  
This one-page infographic explains **what Digital Literacy is** and why it is important for first-year college students. It visually covers five key topics required by the project brief:

- Definition of Digital Literacy  
- Useful digital tools for students  
- Safe internet practices  
- Professional online presence  
- Email etiquette  

**Design Highlights:**  
- Clean, colourful layout with icons and bullet points for easy understanding  
- Made to be shared with batchmates as a quick reference guide
